WHAT IS STEMBASE?

STEMbase is a database to help girls in the United States find opportunities in STEM related camps and activities; especially those who are from areas that do not have a tech culture and their schools do not offer extracurriculars to develop their skills It can be overwhelming to sift through the internet trying to seek out beneficial camps, but with our site, all of this data can be centralized and easy to access for students. This site will help girls find opportunities to gain knowledge in Computer Science as well as different areas of math, physics, etc. Becuase our site revolves around student shared information, other student who browse our site can see different opportunities in the perspective of a fellow peer, ultimatley helping them see if the activity is something they would enjoy.

BE A PART OF STEMBASE!

The site is very simple to use. To browse the different opportunities offered throughout the country, you can go to the opportunties page which is filled with a wide variety of camps and activities to choose from. To upload a camp/activity to share with your fellow peers, go to the upload page where you can fill in information about the activity and express your perspective on the camp to help others choose whether they are interested in that camp or not.
